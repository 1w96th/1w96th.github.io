1 West 96th Street

The church at 1 West 96 is the most historical and architecturally important building in our neighborhood. As you may know, it is one of the precious few buildings in NYC designed by Carrère & Hastings, one of the great Beaux-Arts architectural firms in U.S. history, who designed the historic NY Public Library on 5th Avenue, the Frick Building, and the Met Opera interior, among others.

Although the church is landmarked, the new owners and developers plan to make changes that will compromise the landmarked exterior, and there are a few public meetings that will discuss the changes at which we can voice opposition.

If you are interested and willing to help save one of our greatest neighborhood assets, please join the google group.
